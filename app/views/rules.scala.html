Goal of the Game

Be the first to put all of your tiles from your rack on the field.


Content

  • 104 tiles, with numbers from 1-13, 4 different colours (red, yellow, green, blue)
  • 2 jokers

Every Player receives 14 tiles from the tile-pool. These tiles are placed on the player's rack.


Series of tiles

A "Group" consists of three or four tiles which have the same value, but different colours.
For example: 8 in red, 8 in yellow, 8 in green, 8 in blue.

A "Row" consists of minimum three and maximum 13 tiles with consecutive numerical values, but all must have the same colour.
For example: 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 in red.


Game start

At the first lay out, each player must place at least 30 points with one or more series from his rack on the field.
This turn, the player is not allowed to concatenate a tile with the tiles which are already on the field, or to manipulate a row, or to replace a tile with a joker.
If it is someone's turn and cannot place a tile, he must take a tile from the pool.
Then it is the next players turn.


Manipulate Series

The players try to place as many tiles as possible on the field by rebuilding series or adding tiles to series that are already on the field.
At the end of each turn, only complete series may be on the field and no single tiles may be left over.
No tiles may be put back on the rack of a player.

Series can be manipulated in the following ways:

  • Create new series with one or more tiles from your rack:

    • Tiles on the rack Tiles on the field
    3B 8B
    4B 5B 6B
    8Y 8R 8G
    Player could add the blue 3 to the blue row 4,5,6 and the blue 8 to the group of eights.
    3B 4B 5B 6B
    8Y 8R 8G 8B

  • Use the fourth tile of a group to form a new series:

    • Tiles on the rack Tiles on the field
    3B 5B 6B
    4B 4R 4G 4Y
    A tile is missing to have a valid blue row on the rack. The player takes the blue 4 from the group of four to complete his row.
    3B 4B 5B 6B
    4R 4G 4Y

    • Tiles on the rack Tiles on the field

    1B 1R 1Y

Joker

The joker can replace any tile. As soon as it is used, it automatically takes over the value of the replaced tile.
A joker cannot be exchanged before or during the first time a player put tiles on the field.

Manipulate groups or rows with a joker

Players are allowed to manipulate groups or rows or to add a tile to them, if they contain a joker.
Also it is possible to use a joker by manipulation of a group or a row again,
but it's only allowed to use it in the same turn and not to take it back on the rack.

A joker can be replaced by a tile from your rack or by another suitable tile from the field.
If a joker is removed from a group of three tiles, it can be replaced by a tile in any of the two missing colours.


End of the game

If a player has put all his tiles from his rack on the field the game ends.




Play the game!
